# Policy: Biologic Therapy (TNF Inhibitors) — Prior Authorization Criteria
**Policy ID:** POL-BIO-TNF-001  
**Version:** 3.0  
**Effective Date:** 2024-01-01  

## Overview
Biologic therapy with TNF inhibitors (adalimumab, etanercept, infliximab) 
requires prior authorization for rheumatoid arthritis (RA) indications.

## Approval Criteria
ALL of the following must be documented:

1. **Confirmed diagnosis:** Rheumatoid arthritis confirmed by rheumatologist.
2. **Conventional DMARD failure:** Patient has tried and failed at least 
   TWO conventional DMARDs (e.g. methotrexate, hydroxychloroquine, 
   sulfasalazine, leflunomide) at adequate doses for at least 3 months each.
3. **Lab evidence:** Recent labs (within 6 months) showing:
   - Positive RF or anti-CCP antibody, OR
   - Elevated CRP or ESR consistent with active disease
4. **No contraindications:** No active infection, TB screening completed, 
   hepatitis B screening completed.

## Denial Criteria
Request will be DENIED if:
- Conventional DMARD trial not documented
- Active serious infection present
- TB or hepatitis B screening not completed

## Missing Information — Request More Info If:
- DMARD treatment history incomplete
- Lab results absent or older than 6 months
- TB/hepatitis B screening results not provided
