# Policy: Sleep Study (PSG) — Prior Authorization Criteria
**Policy ID:** POL-SLEEP-001  
**Version:** 1.4  
**Effective Date:** 2024-01-01  

## Overview
Polysomnography (sleep study) requires prior authorization.

## Approval Criteria
ALL of the following must be documented:

1. **Symptoms:** Patient reports at least TWO of the following:
   - Loud snoring reported by bed partner
   - Witnessed apnea episodes
   - Excessive daytime sleepiness (Epworth score >= 10)
   - Morning headaches
   - Difficulty concentrating
2. **Clinical evaluation:** Physician has evaluated the patient and 
   documents clinical suspicion for obstructive sleep apnea (OSA).
3. **Failed alternatives:** Home sleep apnea test (HSAT) was either:
   - Already attempted and inconclusive, OR
   - Clinically inappropriate for this patient with documented reason

## Denial Criteria
Request will be DENIED if:
- Symptoms are not documented
- In-lab PSG requested without attempting HSAT first (unless contraindicated)
- No clinical evaluation on record

## Missing Information — Request More Info If:
- Epworth Sleepiness Scale score not documented
- No record of whether HSAT was attempted
- Physician evaluation notes absent
